Fab Stage 🏭
Wafer Fabrication

Advanced lithography uses extreme ultraviolet light to etch intricate patterns onto silicon wafers. This process can take 4–6 weeks per wafer!
Probe 🔍
Probing and Binning

Post fab, robotic probes conduct electrical tests on every chip. Chips are sorted into 'bins' depending on performance.
Package 📦
Advanced Assembly & Packaging

Cutting edge packaging techniques secure and protect the chips. This enhances performance and improves thermals for the final product.

Supply Chain & Risk Management

Scroll over cards to learn more

🚚 Risk #1
Geopolitical Tensions
Trade restrictions & export bans can disrupt supply.
Mitigation: Diversifying suppliers & strategic stockpiling.
🌐 Risk #2
Global Dependencies
Single-source suppliers like TSMC and ASML increase vulnerability.
Mitigation: Expanding investments in alternatives and building fabs at home, rather than outsourcing.
⚠️ Risk #3
Unexpected Demand Surges
Sudden spikes lead to shortages and long lead times.
Mitigation: Better forecasting with AI & flexible capacity planning.
